Rulebook For VORTEX

  • • VORTEX is an exhilarating, multi-phase competition centered around the Stranger Things universe taking place on 8th September, 2024, pm onwards.
  • • The participants will compete in teams of 3-4 members, with each team appointing a leader as the primary contact with event organisers
  • • The participating teams are required to bring their own laptops for the event.
  • • Teams will face a series of challenges designed to test creativity, problem-solving skills, and teamwork across three phases: Operation Shadow Search , WebFlux and Error 404.
  • • In Phase 1,teams will solve Stranger Things-themed clues and puzzles to advance through a series of locations or tasks, navigating the Upside Down with wit and strategy.
  • • Following this, the teams will design a conceptual themed website using only their imagination and presentation skills.
  • • And finally, for the ultimate challenge, the top teams with the highest cumulative scores from Phases 1 and 2 will advance.
  • • Teams must decipher various formats of clues, morse codes, puzzles to progress and capture the ultimate flag.
  • • Teams must work independently and refrain from collaborating with other teams.
  • • Fair play is expected , any form of cheating or rule violation will lead to disqualification.
  • • The organisers' decisions regarding team assignments, judging, disputes, and rule enforcement are final.
  • • Any disputes or concerns must be raised through the appointed team leader
